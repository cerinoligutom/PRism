1 Welcome

Every PR you touch, in one quiet place.

PRism is a local desktop app that watches every pull request you care about and shows you their real state at a glance — conversation depth, reviewer status, time-in-status. Nothing leaves your machine.

Local-first, read-only

Your PAT lives in the OS keychain. PRism never writes to GitHub — every action opens GitHub in your browser.

Auto-tracking

The moment you comment, get a review request, or are @mentioned, PRism picks it up.

Conversation depth

See unresolved threads, oldest open age, and avg response time — not just "5 comments".
